<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1" class="warning">Summary</a></h2>

<p>TV comedy series narrated by Victor Lewis-Smith, which poked fun at television shows down the years.</p>
<p>Series 1, Episode 3 contained a clip of Lena's appearance on the Golden Shot in 1974, Lewis Smith claimed that Lena was actually a teenage girl disguised as a child.</p>
<p>It also contained a mock shooting of Lena, and a spoof interview with Hughie Green in which crude references to Lena were made.</p>
