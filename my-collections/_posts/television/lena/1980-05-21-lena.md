<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary</a></h2>

<blockquote>
<p>During the opening credits Toast Marster Bryn Williams introduces Lena Zavaroni.</p>
<p>Lena opened the show singing Make Your Own Kind Of Music &amp; Dream A Little Dream, she then introduces Janis Ian whom performs Other Side Of The Sun.</p>
<p>Lena and her dance troupe then perform a song and dance routine Lazy Bones.</p>
<p>Lena then sings Without You after which she introduces David Gates he performs the song Can I Call You.</p>
<p>Lena then sings If A Picture Paints A Thousand Words while David Gates plays guitar.</p>
<p>Lena thanks her Guests and ends the show performing a song and dance routine Jump Shout Boogie..</p>
</blockquote>
