<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary</a></h2>

<blockquote>
<p>During the opening credits Toast Marster Bryn Williams introduces Lena Zavaroni</p>
<p>Lena opened the show singing &quot;I Wanna Be There On The Opening Night&quot;, she then sings &quot;Daydream Believer&quot; Robert White then sings the song &quot;Younger Than Springtime (South Pacific)&quot;</p>
<p>Lena and her dance troupe then perform a song and dance routine (Girl Talk), Lena then sings &quot;longer&quot; after which she introduces Paul Daniels he performs some close up magic using coins</p>
<p>He then performs a sword though the throat illusion with Lena</p>
<p>Lena and her dance troupe then end the show performing a song and dance routine (Rescue Me)</p>
</blockquote>
