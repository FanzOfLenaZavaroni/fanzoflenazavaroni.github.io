<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary of the Article Related To The Aladdin Pantomime</a></h2>
<p>The review covers the Wimbledon Theatre’s 75th‑anniversary pantomime production of Aladdin, produced by Paul Elliott. Sandra Dickinson plays Aladdin, performing alongside her husband Peter Davison as Wishee Washee. David Morton appears as Widow Twankey, Edmund Hockridge provides a strong traditional presence, and George Sewell plays Abanazar. The production features costumes by Val Norton and Bob Norse, with Paul Jury as musical director.</p>
<p>Lena Zavaroni appears as the Princess, overcoming an early microphone issue to take part in the musical numbers and ensemble storytelling. Additional cast members include Bob Carolgees with Spit the Dog as Chinese policemen, Jilly Johnson as the Genie of the Ring, and Carlos Douglas as the Vizier. The reviewer notes that the show offers a wide range of performances for audiences.</p>
