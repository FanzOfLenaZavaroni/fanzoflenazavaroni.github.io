<blockquote>
<p>LENA ZAVARONI: Ma! (He's Making Eyes At Me) (Philips. 6006 3671). Ten-year-old girl who is a regular winner on Opportunity Knocks. She's sure full of confidence, belting it out rather like a young Brenda Lee, or a less young Lulu. Nice booming sort of big- band backing, and a sure fire appeal for the mums and dads - perhaps young fans too. And an old old song as vehicle.</p>
