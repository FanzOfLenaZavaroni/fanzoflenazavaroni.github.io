<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary Of Page 90, The LONDON Section</a></h2>

<p>Capital Radio, a commercial station in London, has chosen not to include the number one single “Billy Don’t Be a Hero” by Paper Lace on its playlist, even though it has been widely played on BBC Radio One and has topped the Music Week charts. The station’s music director, Aiden Day, explains that Capital is also not airing tracks by Lena Zavaroni, Bay City Rollers, Freddie Starr, or Hudson Ford. He says the station focuses on a particular style of music and feels these records do not fit that approach, although he does not clearly define what separates the music they play from the music they leave out.</p>
