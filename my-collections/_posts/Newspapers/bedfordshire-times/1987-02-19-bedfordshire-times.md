<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summery</a></h2>

<p>Singer Lena is to marry her Bedford boyfriend, Peter Wiltshire, after a whirlwind romance. It states that she is “winning the fight against anorexia nervosa.” Peter, who is 24, recently proposed, and the couple plan a white wedding in August, possibly near their London home. He works in London as a recruitment consultant and also sings in pubs at night. Peter says they are very happy and expects many of his old friends will be shocked by the news. His father, David, a Bedford dentist, says the family had seen the relationship becoming serious and are delighted.</p>
