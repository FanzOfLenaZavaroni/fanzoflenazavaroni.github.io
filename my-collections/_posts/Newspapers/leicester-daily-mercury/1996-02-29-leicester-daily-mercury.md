<p>I only have this one line from the article, but it shows how Lena Zavaroni’s name was being used in everyday humour at the time. The line I have is:</p>

<p>“…the gist of the Derby chants centred on Walshie being fat, which seems a harsh indictment coming from anyone other than Lena Zavaroni.”</p>

<p>Even on its own, this bit shows her name being used as a reference point in football banter, which makes it a small example of how she appeared in popular culture.</p>
