<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary</a></h2>

<p>Ten‑year‑old singer Lena Zavaroni is described as rising toward Hollywood fame after being offered the lead role in an upcoming Walt Disney film.</p>
<p>The article notes that she recently achieved major success with her debut record “Ma, He’s Making Eyes at Me.” Despite the attention from film producers, the piece highlights her youth and priorities: she was photographed leaving London, where she lives with her manager, to return home to Rothesay, Scotland, saying she wanted “to see my mummy.”</p>
<p>The article included a photo by Dennis Stone.</p>
