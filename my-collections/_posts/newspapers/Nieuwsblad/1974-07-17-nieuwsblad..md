<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Summary</a></h2>
<p>The Dutch newspaper's article pointed out how since Lena had been discovered she had gone to countries like the US, Denmark and Japan where the labour inspectors do not take a strict view of child labour regulations.</p>
<p>That implication opens up the questions of what the UK inspectors were up to, as even if not in the UK Lena was still a citizen of the UK, so the UK rules would still apply.</p>
<p>The article did not comment on that side of the matter and made no reference to the Solomons, who were the main ones exploiting Lena.</p>
<p>The article claimed Lena's parents had plans to move Lena to the US, possibly Los Angeles, as Lena would have a great chance to do more appearances.</p>
<p>Not covered in the article, but the history of Lena's story now shows that it was more the Solomons who wanted the move than Lena's parents; they may have done, but Victor has been good at not showing his actions in relation to Lena's story, so it remains unconfirmed.</p>
