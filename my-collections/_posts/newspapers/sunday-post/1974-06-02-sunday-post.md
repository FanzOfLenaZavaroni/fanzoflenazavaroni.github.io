<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">No Rest For Lena</a></h2>
<ul>
<li>Lena is due to return home to Rothesay.</li>
<li>She Had been on tour in America for 6 weeks.</li>
<li>The paper states "she wowed some of the top people in show business" they only named Frank Sinatra.</li>
<li>The paper states that after she says "Hello" to her folks, that Lena's priority will be to promote her new record <a href="/discography/singles/1974-05-24-personality-uk">Personality</a>.</li>
</ul>
