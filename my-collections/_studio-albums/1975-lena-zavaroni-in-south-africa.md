<h2 id="infobox4"><a href="#infobox4">Personal Appearances</a></h2>

<p>Lena made two personal appearances at Game Discount World, Durban, South Africa where she signed copies of the Album.</p>

<ul>
<li>28 April 1975 at 12.30 pm, at the Smith Street branch.</li>
<li>29 April 1975 at 3.30pm, at the Ornance Road branch.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Note:</strong> The stores had been open for 5 years in 1975 when Lena made her appearances at the stores.</p>
<p>That is why the advert about Lena had "5 Years Young" with the large 5 also being used as the G in the word Game (5ame).</p>
