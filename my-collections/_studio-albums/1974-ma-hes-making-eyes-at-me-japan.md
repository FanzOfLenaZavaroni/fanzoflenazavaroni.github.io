<h2 id="infobox1"><a href="#infobox1">Album Cover Details</a></h2>
<p>The album cover has a photo of Lena on it with a Obi strip on the left the Japanese on the strip translates as follows, not all the Japanese was readable on the copy of the cover image I have, so this is not a full translation of the Obi strip.</p>
<p><strong>Note on Title:</strong> The Japanese release uses the localised title <strong>ママ、恋かしら</strong>, which translates roughly as <strong>Mama, Is This Love?</strong> This adaptation of the title was created for the Japanese market. The original English title of the song is <strong>Ma! (He’s Making Eyes At Me)</strong>, which was used for the UK and international releases.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Title:</strong>ママ、恋かしら: Mama, Is It Love?</li>
<li><strong>Singer:</strong>リーナ: Lena</li>
</ul>
