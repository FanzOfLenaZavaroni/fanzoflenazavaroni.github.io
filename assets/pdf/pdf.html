8 January 2015 ‘Little Lena’s a Big Girl Now’: Lena Zavaroni and the Anorexic Star Su Holmes (Feminist Media Studies) This is a pre-copyedited, author-produced PDF of an article accepted for publication
Lena Zavaroni became famous as a child star on the British TV talent show Opportunity Knocks in 1974, suffered from anorexia from age thirteen, and subsequently died from complications associated with the problem in 1999, age thirty-five. This article uses 165 press articles from 1974 to 1999 to analyse how Zavaroni's relationship with anorexia was constructed in the British popular press.
